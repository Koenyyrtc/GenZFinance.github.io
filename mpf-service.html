What is MPF?

The Mandatory Provident Fund (MPF) is a compulsory retirement savings scheme in Hong Kong. It aims to assist employees in building up a retirement fund to support their retirement needs.

Importance of Managing MPF:

  • Retirement Support: Managing your MPF effectively ensures that you have sufficient funds to support your retirement lifestyle.
  • Compound Growth: By actively managing your MPF investments, you can potentially benefit from the power of compounding and achieve higher returns over the long term.
  • Risk Mitigation: Monitoring and adjusting your MPF portfolio can help mitigate investment risks and protect your retirement savings.
