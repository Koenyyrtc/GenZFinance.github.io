Tax Deduction Services

Deferred Annuity (QDAP)

Deferred Annuity (Qualifying Deferred Annuity Policy) is one of the most welcomed tax deduction methods in Hong Kong. It allows individuals to reduce their taxable income by contributing to a qualifying deferred annuity plan.

Key features of Deferred Annuity:

  • Limit deductible premium: $60,000 per year
  • Maximum tax deduction effect: $10,200
  • Benefits: Guaranteed deduction with zero risk, the ability to save money for yourself rather than giving it to the government, and potential long-term growth of the annuity fund.

VHIS (Voluntary Health Insurance Scheme)

VHIS is another highly welcomed tax deduction method in Hong Kong. It encourages individuals to obtain certified health insurance plans and provides tax deductions for the premiums paid.

Key features of VHIS:

  • Limit deductible premium: $8,000 per insured person per year
  • Maximum tax deduction effect: $1,360 per insured person
  • Benefits: Guaranteed deduction with zero risk, comprehensive medical coverage, access to quality healthcare services, and peace of mind for unexpected medical expenses.
